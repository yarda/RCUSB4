Wiring of the Aliexpress ArduinoProMicro board:
3: PD0, channel 1
2: PD1, channel 2
RXI: PD2, channel 4
TX0: PD3, channel 3

Connect only channel 2 VCC and GND.

For some reason unknown to me the boards have the port D marked
differently from the datasheet.
